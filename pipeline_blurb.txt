The Education Pipeline for Research in America: research degrees granted by field and sector of work the graduate entered upon graduation, 1996 and 2016.

Is academia declining? Hard to say. Humanities researchers are still 3 times more likely to enter academia than anything else for the past decade. On the flip side, engineers are still 5 times more likely to go into industry than academia. The percentage of life science, physical science and mathematics and computer science graduates entering the ivory tower has decreased, while that of psychology and education graduates has actually increased. University research is here to stay. For now.
